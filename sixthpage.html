Hello all! My name is Arthur Saken. I am a senior at the Brooklyn Studio Seconday High School. For the past couple of years, I have been noticing much litter near the shores. After being a part of an organization that deal with such problems, I organized my own small beach cleanup with my friends and family. Much was collected and lots of people joined as we got to know one another more all while cleaning up our beach with a smile on our faces. After the major cleanup that happened in September, I decided to write an article about it with my friends, who took part in that cleanup. The purpose of this website is to show people what it is that we have done and what we want the ocean to become- a clean place for all of us!
